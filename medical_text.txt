Allergic rhinitis (AR) is an inflammatory disease of the nasal mucosa caused by allergens. Second-generation antihistamines are used to treat AR. Desloratadine (Aerius®) is prescribed in the following dosages: children 1-5 years old — 1.25 mg once daily; children 6-11 years old — 2.5 mg once daily; adults and children over 12 years old — 5 mg once daily. Contraindications: hypersensitivity to the components of the drug.
